Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

1. No Data Collection

SportTahlil AI Assistant does not collect:

  • Personal information (such as names, email addresses, or IP addresses)
  • Sensitive data (such as financial, medical, or personal health details)
  • Cookies or usage logs for tracking purposes

You are free to interact with the assistant anonymously.

2. No Data Sharing

Since we do not collect any personal information, there is no data to share with third parties — including advertisers, analytics platforms, or other services.

3. No User Tracking

SportTahlil AI Assistant does not use cookies, web beacons, or other tracking technologies. Your interaction with the chatbot is not monitored or recorded.

4. Third-Party Services

If you access this assistant through a third-party platform, please be aware that those platforms may collect data independently. We encourage you to review their privacy policies.

5. Changes to This Policy

If our approach to data ever changes, we will update this policy and clearly notify users. Continued use after such changes implies acceptance of the new policy.
