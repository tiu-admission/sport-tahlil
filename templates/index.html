FAQ

Frequently asked
questions

Find answers to common questions about SportTahlil and how our AI-powered performance assistant can help your training.

01What types of performance questions can SportTahlil answer?

SportTahlil covers eight performance domains: physical metrics (VO2 max, speed, power), training load & periodization (ACWR, tapering), nutrition & body composition, mental & cognitive performance, injury prevention & recovery (HRV, return-to-play), competition & tactical analysis, wearable & sensor data, and long-term athlete development.

02Can I upload training data or test reports for analysis?

Yes! You can upload PDF, DOCX, TXT files, and even images of your training logs, lab/fitness test reports, or wearable data exports. Our AI will extract the data, analyze it, and provide detailed feedback on trends, training load, and injury-risk indicators.

03Does SportTahlil replace a coach or doctor?

No. SportTahlil provides general performance guidance for educational purposes. It does not constitute medical advice. For injuries, medical symptoms, or significant changes, we always recommend consulting a qualified sports physician, physiotherapist, or registered dietitian.

04Is my data and conversation history secure?

Yes. Your conversations are encrypted and stored securely. Uploaded documents are automatically deleted after processing. We do not share your data with third parties. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
